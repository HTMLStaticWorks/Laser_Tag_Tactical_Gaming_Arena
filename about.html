ORIGIN STORY

Bringing tactical gaming to real life.

THE MISSION

Founded in 2023 by former game developers and tactical enthusiasts, BattleGrid was built to bridge the gap between digital shooters and physical reality.

We grew tired of the neon-splattered, plastic-gun laser tag of the 90s. We wanted haptic feedback. We wanted objective-based gameplay. We wanted an environment that felt like stepping into a cyberpunk warzone.

Today, BattleGrid represents the pinnacle of live-action entertainment, merging cutting edge infrared technology with meticulously designed physical arenas.
