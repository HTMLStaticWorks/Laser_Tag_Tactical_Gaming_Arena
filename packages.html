FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your time slot and arena sector.

Can we bring our own food?

For Birthday Blitz packages, you may bring your own cake. All other outside food and drink is prohibited unless specifically authorized in a Corporate Assault package.

What if more players show up?

Additional players can be added on the day of the event up to the maximum capacity of your package. They will be charged at the standard "Additional Player" rate.
