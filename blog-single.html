Mastering the VIP Arena: Advanced Strategies

Tactics October 24, 2025 Marcus Vance

The VIP Arena lockout at BattleGrid isn't just about having the space to yourself—it's about utilizing custom game modes that challenge even the most coordinated squads. One of the most popular custom modes is the VIP Escort, where one player is designated the high-value target with no weapon, and the rest of the squad must escort them from Sector Alpha to Sector Omega while the opposing team hunts them down.

Most novice teams fail at VIP Escort within the first three minutes. Why? Because they cluster. When you bunch up around the VIP, you become a single, massive target for a coordinated ambush from the catwalks.

"The most common mistake on the grid is confusing proximity with protection. Give your VIP room to breathe, and establish a 360-degree perimeter."

The Diamond Formation

Instead of clustering, adopt the diamond formation. Place your VIP in the center. Position your most accurate marksman (Point) roughly 20 feet ahead to clear corners. Your two flankers should run parallel on the left and right, maintaining visual contact with the Point but watching the lateral access corridors. Finally, your Rear Guard—ideally someone with excellent spatial awareness—covers the six o'clock position.

This formation allows the squad to react to threats from any direction without crossfire. If the Point encounters heavy resistance, the flankers can pivot and pinch the attackers while the Rear Guard extracts the VIP back to the last secure chokepoint. It requires discipline, but it is the most effective way to traverse the arena.

Next time you book the VIP Arena, assign roles in the briefing room. Stick to the formation, communicate your sightlines, and you might just make it to Sector Omega.
